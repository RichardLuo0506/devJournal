Skills

This blog is a journal to keep track of my progression of skills & interests as a Web Developer. As I learn more from work and personal projects, I hope apply those skills to ideas I previously wasn't ready to execute. In gamer terms, it's like a skill tree of sorts.

Design

Current

I like everything minimalist. I find that the simplest looking designs belie the thought behind them. For most of my work, I use Google's Material Design as a base and add new styles to my personal SASS framework.

Goal

For me, the next step of flat design is texturized flat + animations. I'm very much influnced by video games, and I hope to create experiences that are as intuitive and playful as those of well-made video games.

Misc.

As many of our decisions are made based on emotion, I hope to create designs that evoke certain emotions appropriate for the task.
